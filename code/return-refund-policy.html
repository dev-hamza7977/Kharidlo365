Return, Refund & Exchange Policy

We value your trust in Kharidlo365. Please read our fair and transparent policy to understand how returns, refunds, and exchanges work.

Return Window

Customers can request a return or exchange within 7 days of delivery. Requests made after this period will not be accepted.

Proof Requirement

To process a return or exchange, customers must provide a clear unboxing video or product video as proof showing the issue (damage, wrong product, defect, etc.). Without this, the request cannot be approved.

Eligibility for Returns

Returns are only accepted if:

  • The item is damaged or defective.
  • The wrong item was delivered.
  • The product is not as described on our website.

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, certain items cannot be returned (e.g., personal care, hygiene products, perishables).

Refund Process

Since we only accept Cash on Delivery (COD) orders:

  • Refunds will be sent to the customer’s bank account only.
  • Customers must share their bank details with our support team via email or WhatsApp.
  • Refunds are initiated only after product inspection and approval.
  • Refunds take 5–7 business days to reflect in the customer’s account.

Exchange Policy

If you prefer, instead of a refund, you may request an exchange for the same product (subject to stock availability).

  • Exchange request must be made within 7 days of delivery.
  • Video proof of the issue is mandatory.
  • Exchanges are processed only if the returned item passes inspection.

Cases Where Refunds or Exchanges Are Not Possible

  • Request made after the 7-day return period.
  • No video proof provided.
  • Product damaged due to misuse or negligence by customer.
  • Returned without original packaging, tags, or accessories.
  • Non-returnable categories (as listed above).
